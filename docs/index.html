Job Tracker

A simple, open-source tool to rank and track job applications

View on GitHub →

Rank job openings with customizable scoring formulas based on what matters most to you.

Track Application Status

Follow each application through its full lifecycle—not applied, applied, interviewing, offer, rejected, ghosted, or dumped. Filter by status tabs to focus on what needs your attention right now.

Customizable Scoring

Create scoring formulas that weight the criteria you care about. Prioritize work-life balance, career growth, compensation, or any combination that fits your goals.

Comprehensive Job Details

Track everything about each opportunity across organized tabs: basic info, compensation, work location, and 12 rating criteria like impact, culture, and growth potential. Paste job descriptions from LinkedIn or other sources and HTML is automatically converted to Markdown.

Filter and Sort

Filter by application status with tab-based navigation, narrow down by country or career track, or show only your "wow factor" opportunities. Sort by score or date added.

Job Notes

Keep track of your thoughts, interview feedback, and research for each opportunity. Add, edit, and delete markdown-formatted notes directly from the job list or edit page.

Job Posting Sites

Keep a list of job posting sites and saved search bookmarks. Track when you last checked each one so you never miss new opportunities. One click to mark a site as checked.

Talent Pools

Track company talent pools you've joined or submitted your profile to. Toggle submission status with a click, and keep notes on each pool for quick reference.
